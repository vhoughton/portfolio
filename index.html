Work About CV

test, designer, researcher and sense-maker

what does that mean?

well, think of something you tried to do in the last week, month or year, that was particularly painful.

travelling by train

claiming on insurance

buying a house

my work is all about discovering why it was so painful, by talking to you, and testing out a bunch of ways to make it better.

what sorts of projects interest you?

i'm drawn to work that makes services, products and policies, fairer and kinder. the work I've been most proud of does just that, by helping people:

find missing family

settle as a refugee in a new country

plan for what happens when they die

to make things better, that often means looking at digital solutions and guiding organisations down the tricky path of digital transformation, or looking forward to how we can use new technologies. But sometimes it's less about forms and apps, and more about people, places and culture.

the kind of design I'm interested in takes in that full picture. by taking that bigger picture, we all have a better shot at solving the bigger issues of climate, loneliness, housing and migration, to name a few.

you sound like my mum, but fair point.
