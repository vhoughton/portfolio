About

I help purpose-driven organisations design services that work. I have over 8 years experience designing and delivering products and services across public, private and charity sectors.

I am experienced in the practical work of designing, prototyping and delivering new services, as well as the more foundational work of setting strategy, defining policy, building teams and creative direction. In large, older organisations, this often falls under the title of 'digital transformation'.

I’m interested in working with optimistic organisations solving big problems, and shifting us towards a fairer, brighter future. I'm comfortable in multi-disciplinary roles that are a bit fuzzy around the edges, so if you think I can help, get in touch.
