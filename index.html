About

I help purpose-driven organisations design services that work. I have over 8 years experience designing and delivering products and services across public sector, charity and start-up contexts.

I am experienced in the practical work of designing, prototyping and delivering new services, as well as the more foundational work of setting strategy, defining policy, building teams and creative direction.

I’m interested in working with organisations shifting us towards a fairer world with brighter futures. I'm comfortable in multi-disciplinary roles that are a bit fuzzy around the edges, so if you think I can help, send me an email vickyhp.work@gmail.com or a message on linkedin.
