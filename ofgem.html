Register a heat network

Ofgem, 2023

I worked on a 10 week project to take emerging regulation and policy and prototype what a digital service would be.

We broke the regulatory process down into a service with many stages. The first of which was ‘Register your Heat Network’.

We tested content and flow with users over 4 sprints, refining the designs until ready for development.
