Homes for Ukraine

The brief

Design and deliver the first scheme for UK residents to host refugees in their homes.

My role

Lead designer for the digital delivery team. Converting policy ideas into a vision, a strategy and prototypes, through to a tested service.

Outcome

Over 115,000 Ukrainians have arrived in the UK under the scheme. Our service ensured arrivals and hosts were safe and supported, with routes to finding new accommodation where necessary.

The service

Our service did 2 things:
