Homes for Ukraine

The brief

Design and deliver the first scheme to UK residents to host refugees in their homes.

My role

Lead designer for the digital delivery team. Converting policy ideas into a vision, strategy, and prototype, resulting in a tested service.

Outcome

Over 150,000 Ukrainians have arrived in the UK under the scheme. Our service received almost no defects and was used and updated, with outputs as finding new accommodation where necessary.

The service

1. I ensured the first match was safe, by translating visa applications into new cases for local authorities, where they would manage the safeguarding of both hosts and guests.

2. Found new hosts when the first hosting relationship broke down.
