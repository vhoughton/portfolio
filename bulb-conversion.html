Optimising conversion by reducing error

Bulb, 2019

Approximately 20% of sign-ups to Bulb were stopped due to an ‘enrolment error’

I worked with a data scientist to identify the common causes and ask for extra information from users who face an error to solve the issue up front.

That included introducing inline questioning to resolve errors before submission. We also worked on a javascript free route that involved an accessible modal, rather than dynamic change.
