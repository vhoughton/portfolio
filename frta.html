Family Reunion Travel Assistance (FRTA)

British Red Cross, 2020

I led a discovery to understand why the Family Reunion service was so overwhelmed and over budget.

Problem: Caseworkers are unable to turn people away when they ask for help.

Solution: Reframing the service as a grants application process, where there are finite spaces.

This included developing tools so caseworkers could check applications in each area.
