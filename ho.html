Mapping risks at the UK border

Home office (ukgov), 2020

The Home Office has a long-term project replacing the technology that tracks and responds to risks at UK borders. The project is made up of 8 teams that focus on a specific part of the technology.

There was a gap in understanding what the back-end processes would look like. With a user researcher we did a discovery and an alpha phase exploring internal tooling for processing applications.
